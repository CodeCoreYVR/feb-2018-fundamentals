Gramma's Pho Recipe

Ingredients

  • Rice Noodle
  • Beef Bones
  • Thinly Sliced Beef
  • Fresh Herbs

Instructions

  1. Boil the bones in water for 3 days
  2. Add the noodles
  3. Add the beef slices
  4. Add the herbs and serve
